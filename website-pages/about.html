About The Birdcare Company

The Birdcare Company is the UK’s market leader in cage and aviary bird supplements, serving approximately three-quarters of the serious bird breeder market. This success is the result of a combination of technical excellence and outstanding customer service. Their achievements have been recognized through multiple awards, including the FSB Award in 1997 and the Gloucester Citizen Award in 2001.

The company’s philosophy is to work closely with bird keepers to understand their challenges and then search globally for innovative ingredients to solve those problems. They pride themselves on developing comprehensive, easy-to-use products with proven effectiveness.

This technical excellence is supported by a free advisory service available to both retailers and consumers. Support is accessible via phone (0845 130 8600 – local call charge only), email (advice@BirdcareCo.com), or their extensive and informative website.

The Birdcare Company produces supplements to maintain bird health (for both pets and breeding stock), help sick birds recover, and boost breeding performance. It's not uncommon for breeders to double their production after incorporating The Birdcare Company’s system into their birds’ diet. Many of their products are used, recommended, and sold by the UK’s leading avian veterinarians.
